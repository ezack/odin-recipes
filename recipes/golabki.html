Stuffed Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients

  • Large cabbage head
  • 1.3 lbs minced meat
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 8-12 oz boullion
  • Salt, Pepper to taste

Sauce

  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • Salt, Pepper, Sugar to taste
  • 1 heaping tablespoon butter
