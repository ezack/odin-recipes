Slow Cooker Crack Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4-6 slices cooked thick-cut bacon
  • 3 lbs boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 8oz blocks cream cheese
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • dices green onion (optional)

Cooking directions

  1. Cook bacon until slightly crispy, chop into small pieces and set aside
  2. In slow cooker, place chicken thighs, cream cheese, and seasonings in layers
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 4 hours, until chicken shreds easily
  4. Once chicken shreds, use a large fork to shred and combine ingredients
  5. Top with bacon bits (opt. green onion) while still hot
