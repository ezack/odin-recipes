Crab and Rice Primavera

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups BirdsEye Broccoli, Green Beans, Pearl Onions and Red Peppers
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 3/4 lb imitation crabmeat or crabmeat chunks
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon basil
  • 1 1/2 cups Minute Rice
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  1. Bring vegetables and water to boil in saucepan, stirring occasionally
  2. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 3 minutes
  3. Add milk, crabmeat, butter, garlic and basil
  4. Bring to full boil
  5. Stir in rice and cheese
  6. Cover; remove from heat
  7. Let stand 5 minutes
  8. Fluff with fork

Makes 4 servings
