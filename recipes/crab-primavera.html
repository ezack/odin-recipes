Crab and Rice Primavera


Ingredients

    1 1/2 cups BirdsEye Broccoli, Green Beans, Pearl Onions and Red Peppers
    1/4 cup water
    1 1/3 cups milk
    3/4 lb imitation crabmeat or crabmeat chunks
    2 tablespoons butter
    1 teaspoon garlic powder
    3/4 teaspoon basil
    1 1/2 cups Minute Rice
    1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    Bring vegetables and water to boil in saucepan, stirring occasionally
    Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 3 minutes
    Add milk, crabmeat, butter, garlic and basil
    Bring to full boil
    Stir in rice and cheese
    Cover; remove from heat
    Let stand 5 minutes
    Fluff with fork

Makes 4 servings
